ABOUT ADOPTING

QUESTIONS TO ASK BEFORE ADOPTING

  • Am I ready to make a commitment to caring for a rescued cat for its lifetime, including food, shelter, regular vet care, and possibly unexpected/ emergency vet care?
  • Am I able to devote the time and patience required to transition a cat into my home?
  • Do I acknowledge and understand that a rescued pet may need a concerted effort to learn how to behave in its new environment?
