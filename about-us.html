BOARD MEMBERS

Cathy Stone
Founder & President

Cathy leads Houston's most effective cat protection organization. She founded the Third Ward Cat Committee in 2015 after encountering many stray cats near her work in the downtown Houston area.

Anne Lee
Secretary

Anne provides all the behind-the-scene works of the Third Ward Cat Committee. She leads programmatic works to further advance animal protection as well as managing inventory and documentation.

Austin Garcia
Treasurer

Austin leads financial and administrative systems and processes in the Third Ward Cat Committee. Prior to joining TWCC, he spent 5 years as a financial manager at The Houston Nature Conservancy.
